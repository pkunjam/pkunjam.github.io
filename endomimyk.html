EndoMIMYK

  • Supervisor: Dr. Shanthanu Chakravarthy & Dr. Nithin Shivashankar
  • Institution: Mimyk Medical Simulations, Indian Institute of Science

An endoscopy simulator combines haptics, design, and virtual reality to offer an immersive system for doctors to practice and learn endoscopy procedures.

  • I created and built 3D interactive training modules for the simulator.
  • I implemented the "accessory channel and tools" feature, and formulated the workflow for three associated tools: the biopsy tool, snare tool, and sclerotherapy needle.
  • I utilized Blender to generate fundamental 3D models and crafted animations for the doctor and various tools, each serving distinct purposes in the 3D model.
Due to confidentiality, I am not privileged to share the work results during my internship at the Mimyk Medical Simulations. Please consider reaching out to Dr. Shanthanu or Dr. Nithin for details.
