Game Oasis Hackathon

I got selected to join Game Oasis Hackathon 2019 held in Bangalore. One of the largest global gaming hackathons co-hosted with Binance Labs, Contentos, Harmony, and Matic Network.

I worked on a project based on "Smart Contracts in Blockchain" with a team of three people. I utilized and modified one of my previous projects- "2D racing," a small racing game made in Unity from scratch. The idea was to create a game where two persons bet on cars, and the car that collects the most bounties on its way to the finishing line wins. The winner's ID is automatically sent to the blockchain network through the Node.js server to make the smart transactions.
